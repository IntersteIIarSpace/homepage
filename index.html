Story

InterstellarSpace was founded in August 2024 by a group of passionate students with a common interest in space technology. Our goal is to build satellites and eventually rockets to promote learning and technological development among students.

Team

We are a team of over 15 members, all with different skills in engineering, physics, computer science, and more. We work together to realize our ambitious projects.

Values

We value collaboration, innovation, and learning. Our environment encourages members to be curious and explore new ideas while we work towards common goals.

Projects

Satellite

We are currently working on developing our first satellite. This project provides students with hands-on experience in space technology.

Propulsion

Our long-term goal is to build a satellite with an advanced propulsion system. This will challenge our members to combine theory and practice in innovative ways.
